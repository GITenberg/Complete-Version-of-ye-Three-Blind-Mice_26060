Three Small Mice

Three Small Mice

“Good evening, Host, can you give us a bed?”

Three Cold Mice

Woke up next morn

Searched for some food

Searched for some food

Who had eaten the nut they could not tell,

These three Hungry Mice.

These three Starved Mice.

Ate all they could

Three Scared Mice

Ran for their lives

Ran for their lives

These three Scared Mice.

Three Sad Mice

What could they do?

What could they do?
